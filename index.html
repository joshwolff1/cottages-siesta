THE COTTAGES

A STAY IN PARADISE

  • WE OFFER AN EXPERIENCE

    Rates as low as under $200/night*

    The Siesta Key Cottages are vibrant, festive, newly decorated 2 BED/2 BATH (sleeps 6) pool homes. They are within 4 minutes walking distance to the gorgeous Siesta Key beach access (no major roads to cross). Point of Rocks, the closest area of the beach, is the most notable beach access for sunbathing, paddleboarding, kayaking, snorkeling and fishing because of the amazing natural limestone formation that juts out across a shallow soft sanded bottom.

    Private porches and seating in the lighted, lush, tropical landscape are available for those quiet moments everyone needs. You’ll struggle with the decision to go down to the country’s #1 rated beach, chill in the pool, or go to the Village, all within easy walking or biking distance.

    Includes everything. Rates vary based on time and availability.

  • WE OFFER AN OPPORTUNITY

    The cottages have exclusive access to the heated pools with a “sun shelf”. The interior consists of beautiful new décor, flat-panel, wall-mounted TVs in all bedrooms and living areas, Netflix, Roku, and Pandora entertainment. Fine linens, towels and everything else are truly “first-class” here. Private washers / dryers and barbeque grills are available for your convenience in each unit. Each cottage has their own “toy” closet filled with an array of complimentary bicycles and beach equipment. Free private parking is also included.

    Marc Wolff, the owner, is always a phone call away and happily willing to help you discover all that Siesta Key has to offer during your vacation. Everything you need for a wonderful experience is here!

THE SURROUNDING AREA

The surrounding area is rife with liveliness and island vibes. It's packed with nightlife, restaurants, boating, and the #1 beach in the nation! The free Siesta Key Trolley drives right by the cottages. You can be picked up or dropped off at any time.

BBQ AND GRILL

Each unit has its own barbecue grill. On those chilly nights, don’t forget about the fire pit as well!

complimentary bicycles

Each unit has complimentary bicycles. Cruise all around Siesta Key on your beach cruisers!

heated pool area

Each cottage has their own heated pool area, perfect for cooling off and a setting more private than the beach. Each pool area has a full set of tables, chairs and umbrellas. So relax, throw in some of the beach toys, and jump in!

free parking in the center of everything

Parking on Siesta Key can be quite difficult because of limited space. We take care of that with free parking spaces right in front of the cottages. With 15 spots and only 4 units, parking will never be a problem!

full kitchen

All cottages include a fully loaded kitchen, complete with a stove, oven, spacious countertop, utensils, microwave, refrigerator, freezer, etc.
